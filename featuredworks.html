Design & Illustration Portfolio (Visual)

Welcome to my graphic design and illustration portfolio. This is a collection of selected works from my tenure at Envirofriendly Printing, ICAST, and work that I've done for musical groups around the Denver area. The portfolio spans the past 15 years and hopefully shows a wide variety of techniques, ideas, and styles. In most cases, the time and production budgets for these projects was minimal so creativity was paramount. Thank you for your interest and enjoy your look around.
Please click the photos below for more information and examples
ICAST: Weld County Campaign
A location targeted marketing campaign for my former employer requiring the following actions: database building, market research, email campaigns, case studies, printed collateral, and data interpretation.
Miscellaneous Concert Poster Illustrations
A collection of both traditional and digitally illustrated posters for some of the artists around the Denver area that I have worked with.
Pinwheel Advertising: Identity and Collateral
An identity package for a Colorado manufacturing company making roadside attention grabbers. Work includes: Identity, business papers, leave behinds, order forms.
CAPS Mental Health & DUI Services: Identity Design
An identity design for a Mental Health and DUI counceling service. Work included a business paper set, branding, and signage.
Miscellaneous Identity Design Packages & Collateral
A collection of miscellaneous branding solutions I've developed as well as collateral to go along with them. Items include business paper sets, labels, flyers & one sheets, t-shirts and more.
Bill Hopping for Littleton City Council
Branding identity for a Littleton city council candidate including yard signs, mailing pieces and all associated mailing services.
Littleton Cafe: Menu Design
a ground up design of a menu for a quaint greasy spoon diner in the Denver suburbs, including food photography and set dressing.
Littleton Print & Copy: Identity Package
Branding identity and all related collateral for the retail arm of my former employer, a small town 4 color print shop. Includes business papers, rack cards, signage, sales marketing, giveaways and website imagery.
Spokeshaver: CD Packaging and Concert Poster illustration
All of the marketing projects I created for the rock band SpokeShaver. including: record sleeve artwork, concert poster illustration, social media management, audio production, and other misc. collateral.
Great American House Fire Poster and Record Design
All of the marketing projects I created for the rock band Great American House Fire including: record sleeve artwork, concert poster illustration, social media management, sticker design, audio production, and other misc. collateral.
